Your privacy matters

Clear, simple explanations of what we collect, why and how we protect it.

Overview

JourneyScape is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the information necessary to provide services, communicate with you and improve our offerings.

Information We Collect

We may collect contact details, booking preferences, payment metadata (handled via secure providers) and usage data (pages visited, form interactions). We do not store full payment card numbers.

How We Use Information

Data is used to process bookings, send confirmations, respond to enquiries and personalize recommendations. With your consent we may send occasional marketing messages.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve site function, remember preferences and measure performance. You can manage cookie settings via your browser.

Third Parties

We share information with trusted partners (hotels, transport, payment processors) strictly to fulfil bookings. Partners must follow applicable data protection rules.

Data Security

We apply technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. While we work to secure information, no system is completely risk-free.
